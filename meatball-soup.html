Meatball Soup

  • Onion
  • Carrot
  • Red Pepper
  • Potatoes
  • Water
  • Seasoning(salt, bay leaf, coriander, salt, black pepper, parsley)
  • White Bread
  • Groumd Meat

Step 1: Mix onion, carrot, and red pepper. Sear in oil.

Step 2: Cut potatoes into cubes.

Step 3: Heat a large pot with water, salt, bay leaf, coriander, a small amount of onion and carrot, and the potatoes.

Step 4: Soak 2 slices of white bread in water.

Step 5: Mix ground meat, the bread, salt, black pepper, coriander. Mix thoroughly.

Step 6: Make small meatballs and immediately put them in the boiling soup.

Step 7: Place the seared onions and carrots into the soup. Add parsley.

Step 8: Once the meatballs float, cook on low heat for 5-7 minutes.

Step 9: Serve.
