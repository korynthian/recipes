Banana-Blueberry Bread

  • 3/4 cup Kefir
  • 2-4 bananas
  • 1 egg
  • 4 teaspoons butter
  • 4 cups bread flour
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 teaspons yeast
  • 1/2 cup blueberries, dried or fresh

Step 1: Slice bananas.

Step 2: In a blender, combine the sliced bananas, kefir, egg, and sugar.

Step 3: Once the banana mixture is smooth, mix it with the flour, salt, yeast, and blueberries.

Step 4: Melt the butter in a pan. Once liquid, add to the dough. The dough should be quite liquid.

Step 5: Pour the dough into a baking dish. Bake at 350 until desired readiness.

Step 6: Cool and serve.
