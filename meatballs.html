Meatballs

  • Ground Meat
  • Chopped Onion
  • Spices (salt, coriander, and pepper)
  • 2-3 slices of white bread
  • Water
  • One egg yolk
  • Flour
  • Oil

Step 1: Mix ground meat, chopped onion, and spices (salt, coriander, and pepper) to taste.

Step 2: Soak 2-3 slices of white bread in water.

Step 3: Run all the ingredients through a meat grinder.

Step 4: Add one egg yolk to the meat. Mix well until the egg has been beaten into the meat.

Step 5: Let the meat sit for 10 minutes.

Step 6: Heat a pan with oil.

Step 7: Shape the meat into balls. Cover the balls in flour.

Step 8: Fry the meatballs in the pan for 7-10 minutes on low heat without a lid.

Step 9: Flip the meatballs and fry for another 7-10 minutes with the lid on.

Step 10: Cool and serve.
