Chicken Noodle Soup

    • Water
    • Chicken Thighs
    • Onion
    • Carrot
    • Oil
    • Spices (salt, black pepper, coriander, bay leaves)
    • Noodles

    Step 1: Make stock with chicken thighs, half an onion, and half a carrot.

    Step 2: While the stock is cooking, fry onion and shredded carrots for ten minutes.

    Step 3: The stock should be ready in 30-40 minutes. When the stock is ready, Add the fried carrots and onion to the stock.

    Step 4: Season. Add salt, black pepper, coriander, and bay leaves.

    Step 5: Bring back to boil and add noodles. Cook 6-10 minutes.

    Step 6: Serve.
